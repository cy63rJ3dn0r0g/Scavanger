biography

Nađa Pijović is a talented artist and bookbinder born and raised in Belgrade, Serbia. With a deep passion for leather from an early age, she developed a profound appreciation for the smell and texture of fresh leather. Nađa's artistic journey led her to pursue her studies at the Academy of Art, specializing in bookbinding. As a bookbinder, Nađa combines her love for drawing, craftsmanship, and the art of bookmaking. Her intricate and unique creations showcase her attention to detail and dedication to her craft. Each book she creates is a work of art, meticulously handcrafted with precision and creativity. Nađa finds joy and fulfillment in the process of bringing together various materials, binding techniques, and her artistic expression to breathe life into every book she creates. Her passion for books extends beyond their physicality; she believes that books hold the power to transport, inspire, and connect people. Through her artistic journey, Nađa Pijović continues to explore the boundless possibilities of bookbinding, pushing the boundaries of creativity and craftsmanship. Her dedication and passion for her art form are evident in every piece she creates, capturing the essence of her artistic spirit and love for the timeless art of bookmaking.
